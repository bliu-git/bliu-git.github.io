About Me

I am a research scientist with a Ph.D. in Quantitative Measurement. I focus on constructing measurement tools and conducting survey research using psychometric and statistical analysis for data-driven insights with R and Qualtrics. My work experience includes roles in both academia and industry, involving research on educational measurement, talent assessment, and customer behavior analysis. I have collaborated with cross-functional teams to translate technical and business requirements into actionable insights in both academic and business environments.
